Exploratory Data Analysis

Understanding the data before modeling

Exploratory analysis was conducted to understand distributions, detect anomalies, and validate assumptions prior to scoring.

Data Sources

Public datasets from Quebec government portals covering rental prices, safety indicators, education availability, and infrastructure.

Key Insights

  • Rental price outliers concentrated in central boroughs
  • School availability correlated with family-oriented areas
  • Safety indicators varied significantly across locations

Impact on Modeling

Findings from EDA influenced normalization strategies, weighting decisions, and feature selection in the final model.

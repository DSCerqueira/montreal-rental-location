Stakeholder Register and RASCI Matrix

Stakeholder Groups

For this project there will be 4 types of stakeholders:

  • Primary stakeholders (End users): Renters, Landlords, Real Estate Agencies
  • Secondary stakeholders (Indirect users and influencers): Municipal & Public Sector Organizations
  • Technical stakeholders: Data Analysts, Data Engineers, BI Developers
  • Data Providers: Statistics Canada, City of Montreal Open Data, Quebec Open Data, Kijiji, Walkscore, HeiGit
